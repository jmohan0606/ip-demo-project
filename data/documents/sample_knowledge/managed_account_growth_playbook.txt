Managed Account Growth Playbook

Purpose: Help advisors identify households with high cash balances, low managed revenue penetration,
and appropriate risk profile for a managed account review.

Recommended signals:
- Household AUM above 500,000
- Cash balance above 75,000
- Managed revenue percentage below peer average
- Recent client review meeting completed
- Moderate, balanced, or growth risk profile

Compliance reminder: Recommendations must be suitable, documented, and aligned to risk profile.
