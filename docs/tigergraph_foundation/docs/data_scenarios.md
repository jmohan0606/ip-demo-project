# Deterministic Sample Data Scenarios

The sample package is designed to populate every planned screen and demonstrate meaningful variation rather than uniform generated records.

## Organization and personas

- 1 firm, 3 divisions, 6 regions, 12 markets and 24 branches.
- 60 advisors distributed through branches and markets.
- Complete DDW → RDW → MDW → Advisor reporting relationships.
- Executive, DDW, RDW, MDW, Advisor, AGP Advisor, Compliance, Admin and AI Ops users.
- Persona scope edges for firm, division, region, market, branch and advisor access.

## Client, product and performance data

- 360 households and 720 accounts.
- 64 products organized under categories and subcategories.
- Diversified account-product holdings and eligibility records.
- 24 monthly periods.
- 10,080 revenue transactions plus monthly AUM, NCF, NNM and product-revenue records.
- Intentionally varied top, middle and bottom performers and positive/negative trends.

## AGP program

- One 24-month AGP program.
- Eight milestones at 3-month increments.
- 24 AGP enrollments at different program stages.
- Eight milestone-progress records per enrollment.
- Five KPI measurements per milestone progress.
- Completed, on-track, at-risk and upcoming milestone states.
- Goals, coaching sessions and manager reviews.

## CRM

- CRM activity linked to advisors, households and accounts.
- 300 leads with pending, completed, overdue and converted examples.
- 180 referrals with pending, completed, overdue and converted examples.
- 180 CRM opportunities with open, won and lost outcomes.
- Qualification, proposal, negotiation, closed-won and closed-lost stages.
- High-value, stalled and overdue cases supporting AGP and coaching screens.

## AI simulation artifacts

- Versioned feature snapshots for advisors, households, accounts and products.
- Deterministic embeddings and similarity matches.
- Prediction results for performance, AGP, household/account and recommendation outcomes.
- AI opportunities and recommendations linked to features, predictions, targets and reasoning.
- All four severities: Info, Attention, Urgent and Critical.
- Estimated impact, confidence, priority and due-date variation.

## Memory, explanation and learning

- Semantic, episodic, preference, outcome and reasoning memories.
- Conversation turns and context relationships.
- Reasoning traces and graph-evidence relationships.
- Accept, reject, defer, not-relevant and complete feedback actions.
- Outcomes and learning signals linked back to recommendations.
- Agent executions, tool calls, evaluations and guardrail events.

Automated evidence for these scenarios is generated by `scripts/validate_business_scenarios.py` and written to `reports/business_scenario_validation.md`.
